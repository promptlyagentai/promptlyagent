# **Client Snapshot**

## Client Objectives & Strategic Roadmap

1. **Client's Organizational Mission & Objectives**:
    - **tl;dr:** Regional financial services institution providing banking, wealth management, and investment services to 200,000+ customers across 12 states with focus on digital transformation and cybersecurity excellence
    - Sterling Financial Services operates 180 branch locations with $42 billion in assets under management. Their mission centers on delivering secure, accessible financial services through modern digital channels while maintaining personalized relationship banking. Core focus areas include cloud infrastructure modernization, zero-trust security architecture, and digital banking platform reliability.
2. **Current business objectives include:**
    - Migrate core banking infrastructure from on-premise data centers to hybrid cloud architecture
    - Achieve 99.99% uptime for digital banking platforms (mobile app, online banking)
    - Implement comprehensive infrastructure monitoring and incident response capabilities
    - Meet stringent regulatory compliance requirements (SOC 2, PCI-DSS, FFIEC)
    - Reduce infrastructure operational costs by 30% through cloud optimization
    - Modernize disaster recovery and business continuity capabilities
