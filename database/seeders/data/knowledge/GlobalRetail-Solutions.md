# **Client Snapshot**

## Client Objectives & Strategic Roadmap

1. **Client's Organizational Mission & Objectives**:
    - **tl;dr:** Leading omnichannel retail experience provider focused on seamless integration between physical stores and digital commerce platforms
    - GlobalRetail Solutions operates 450+ stores across North America with aggressive digital transformation goals. Their mission centers on creating frictionless shopping experiences that blend in-store and online channels. Core focus areas include personalization at scale, inventory optimization, and customer data platform integration.
2. **Current business objectives include:**
    - Complete migration from legacy Magento platform to modern headless CMS architecture
    - Achieve 40% year-over-year growth in e-commerce revenue
    - Implement AI-driven product recommendations and personalization
    - Reduce cart abandonment rate from 68% to below 55%
    - Establish real-time inventory visibility across all channels
    - Launch mobile-first shopping experience with AR try-on capabilities

### **Client Strategic Business Roadmap**

GlobalRetail's strategic roadmap emphasizes digital-first customer experiences while maintaining operational excellence across physical retail locations. The organization is executing a two-year digital transformation program that includes platform modernization, data infrastructure consolidation, and advanced analytics implementation.
