# **Client Snapshot**

## Client Objectives & Strategic Roadmap

1. **Client's Organizational Mission & Objectives**:
    - **tl;dr:** Regional healthcare provider network delivering accessible, patient-centered care through 85 clinics and 3 hospital systems with emphasis on digital health innovation
    - HealthFirst Medical Group serves over 500,000 patients across the Northeast with a mission to transform healthcare delivery through technology. Core focus areas include telemedicine expansion, patient engagement platforms, integrated care coordination, and health data interoperability. The organization is recognized as a leader in value-based care models and digital health adoption.
2. **Current business objectives include:**
    - Launch comprehensive patient portal with appointment scheduling, medical records access, and telemedicine capabilities
    - Achieve 70% patient portal adoption rate within 18 months
    - Reduce appointment no-show rates from 18% to below 10% through automated reminders and engagement
    - Implement FHIR-compliant data exchange with external health systems
    - Meet strict HIPAA compliance requirements across all digital touchpoints
    - Improve patient satisfaction scores (HCAHPS) by 15 points

### **Client Strategic Business Roadmap**

HealthFirst's strategic roadmap prioritizes patient experience transformation while maintaining rigorous security and compliance standards. The organization is investing heavily in digital health infrastructure to support population health management, care coordination, and value-based payment models. Key initiatives focus on reducing administrative burden, improving care access, and enabling data-driven clinical decision support.
