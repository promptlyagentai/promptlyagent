## Project Overview

Consolidate 200+ disparate university websites into unified WordPress VIP multisite platform with custom theme framework, governance model, and comprehensive training program. Enable distributed content management while maintaining brand consistency and accessibility standards.

## Business Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. Consolidate 200+ independent websites into unified platform
2. Increase undergraduate applications by 20% through improved digital experience
3. Reduce technical debt and operational overhead by 40%
4. Establish consistent brand identity across all digital properties
5. Empower academic departments with self-service content management
6. Meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance university-wide

### Success Metrics
- All 200+ sites migrated by June 2027
- Content editor satisfaction >8/10
- Accessibility compliance >95% (automated testing)
- Site performance >90 Lighthouse scores
- Enrollment website conversion rate +15%
- IT support tickets reduced by 50%

## Migration Approach

### Site Migration Process (Per Site)

**Week 1: Discovery & Planning**
- Stakeholder kickoff meeting
- Content audit
- URL mapping
- Custom functionality assessment
- Training scheduling

**Week 2-3: Migration Execution**
- Content migration (automated + manual QA)
- Media migration and optimization
- Design customization
- Custom functionality build (if needed)
- Redirect implementation
- SEO validation

**Week 4: Training & Launch**
- Content editor training (2-hour session)
- UAT with stakeholders
- Final QA and accessibility audit
- Launch preparation
- DNS cutover
- Post-launch monitoring

**Week 5+: Stabilization**
- Post-launch support
- Content refinement
- Analytics setup
- SEO monitoring
