# **Client Snapshot**

## Client Objectives & Strategic Roadmap

1. **Client's Organizational Mission & Objectives**:
    - **tl;dr:** Boutique hotel and resort management company operating 28 luxury properties across North America with focus on direct booking growth and personalized guest experiences
    - Meridian Hospitality Group curates distinctive luxury accommodations emphasizing local character and exceptional service. Their mission centers on creating memorable guest experiences while building direct customer relationships to reduce OTA dependency. Core focus areas include direct booking optimization, guest personalization, property management system modernization, and unified digital presence across diverse properties.
2. **Current business objectives include:**
    - Launch unified booking platform across all 28 properties with personalized recommendations
    - Increase direct bookings from 42% to 65% of total reservations
    - Reduce OTA commission expenses by $3.2M annually
    - Implement guest preference management and loyalty program
    - Achieve 9.0+ average guest satisfaction score (currently 8.4)
    - Launch property management tools for revenue optimization

# **Account Risk Plan**

Meridian Hospitality Group represents a strategic hospitality partnership with exceptional business impact through direct booking growth and strong potential for loyalty, revenue management, and property expansion services. The combined platform development, SiteWatch, and InfraWatch demonstrates comprehensive digital partnership value.

Key risk factors include:

- **Direct Booking Revenue Dependency**: Platform reliability directly impacts millions in revenue; any downtime critical
- **OTA Rate Parity Compliance**: Careful management of pricing and availability across channels required
- **Seasonal Traffic Spikes**: Platform must handle peak booking periods without performance degradation
- **Property Stakeholder Management**: 28 properties means diverse stakeholder needs and change management complexity
